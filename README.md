# Daffodil Cottage Co. Pet Services Analytics Portfolio

## Project Overview

This project analyzes a realistic relational dataset for **Daffodil Cottage Co.**, a pet services and dog training business. The goal is to show how SQL and Tableau can turn day-to-day service records into business insights about revenue, profitability, client acquisition, service demand, and growth opportunities.

The dataset was designed as a recruiter-facing analytics project and includes related tables for clients, dogs, services, transactions, calendar dates, city zones, and marketing campaigns.

## Business Questions

1. Which service categories generate the most revenue and gross profit?
2. Which acquisition channels bring in the highest-value clients?
3. Which city zones have the strongest client and revenue concentration?
4. How do revenue and service demand trend over time?
5. Which clients or service types need follow-up, retention attention, or operational support?

## Key Findings

- **Training** is the largest revenue driver, generating **$57,020.92** in revenue and **$40,703.58** in gross profit.
- **Referral** is the top acquisition channel by revenue, generating **$20,430.82** across **27** clients.
- **Portland** is the highest-revenue city zone, generating **$16,627.19** across **17** clients.
- The dataset supports analysis across profitability, client value, marketing ROI, geographic demand, and operational workload.

## Tools Used

- **SQL** for joins, aggregations, client segmentation, revenue analysis, and window functions

## Recommended Tableau Dashboards

1. **Executive Overview** — revenue, profit, transactions, clients, and monthly trends
2. **Service Performance** — service category revenue, gross profit, margins, and demand
3. **Client & Dog Insights** — client value segments, household types, dog behavior needs
4. **Marketing & Geography** — acquisition channels, campaign performance, and city-zone map
5. **Operations View** — follow-ups, pending payments, trainer hours, and travel miles
